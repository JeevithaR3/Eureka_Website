Themes & Focus Areas

Develop impactful tech solutions aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs):

Clean Energy & Solar Innovation
Smart Agriculture & Rural Tech
Water Conservation & Wastewater Management
Carbon Capture & Circular Economy
Urban Sustainability & Smart Cities
Climate Resilience with AI & IoT
Eco-friendly Transportation & Mobility

Event Schedule

Day 1: Ideation & Build

  • Problem statements reveal & mentor introduction
  • Opening ceremony with dignitaries from REVA, SRM & Industry
  • Round-1 ideation & validation
  • Build phase initiation with checkpoints
  • Evening cultural night & networking

Day 2: Final Build & Awards

  • Final build & refinement phase
  • Evaluation by jury panel (industry professionals, startup founders, professors)
  • Live demo & pitching
  • Award ceremony & closing remarks

Dates: September 3–4, 2025
