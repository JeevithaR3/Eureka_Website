Where Innovation Meets Sustainability

Hack for Earth by Club Eureka is more than a hackathon—it's a national movement to empower the next generation of innovators, technologists, and changemakers. Our mission is to unite brilliant minds from across India to tackle real-world sustainability challenges through creativity, collaboration, and cutting-edge technology.

Why Participate?

  • Impact: Build solutions that address the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs).
  • Mentorship: Work with industry experts, startup founders, and academic leaders.
  • Recognition: Compete for a ₹3,00,000 prize pool, internships, and national media coverage.
  • Community: Join a vibrant network of students, professionals, and innovators.
  • Experience: Enjoy a blend of tech, culture, and networking in a two-day immersive event.

Our Vision

We believe in the power of youth-driven innovation to create a sustainable future. Hack for Earth is a platform to turn ideas into action, foster eco-consciousness, and inspire solutions that matter.
